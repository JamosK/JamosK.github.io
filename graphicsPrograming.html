Graphics Programming
Graphics Programming is like painting with numbers. And I have tried to use it to painting some unique graphics.
  I found out about Graphics programming in the spring of 2024. And since then have tinkered with it whenever I get a idea I want to create. As what Graphics Programming is the coding of shaders for the gpu, which gives it applications in visual generation and real time interaction.

What called me to it was the potential for the programming of truly new graphical effects. As I know blender, and blender gives access to 1000s of potential shading, and modeling techniques. But what blender can't give is something truly out there and unique.

  I developed my fundamental understanding by following CatLikeCoding Graphics Programming tutorials for Unity. But those were fundamentally tutorials.
So after completing a majority of the CatLikeCoding's projects, I started creating my own.
