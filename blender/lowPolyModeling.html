2023/9/22 "Baked Lighting Experiments"
This is from one time I tried out rendering with baked lighting. The idea of baked lighting is instead of doing real time ray traced mathematics to find what a scene looks like, you do that math and save the result in textures. And then just re-render the scene using those textures. The result I got was ok, you can see some issues at the borders of objects. And it appears I didn't use a Denoiser, maybe this is because I thought it looked cool. I also really faced the reality of the massive texture size necessary for this rendering to look good. I also learned your lighting has to be very diffuse for the result to look passable.
