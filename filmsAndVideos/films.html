Films
I first got into film proper during highschool, when I entered a art program called Freestyle Academy. Before I had made many small videos and memes on my won, but this program taught me how to create comprehensive projects. The biggest thing this program did was give us 12 hours of class time each week to work on film. Which for school is a absurd amount of time to spend in a singular class.

But with a absurd amount of time, you can learn a absurd amount.
The class covered just about everything about film, screen writing, directing, cinematography, production design, location scouting, audio design, color grading, and the list goes on.
All of those skills combined in the senior narrative project, which require writing and filming a film.



Another notable assignment was documentary, as it taught a completely set of skills for documenting a story rather then telling one. I really enjoyed this process of documenting a story, and then sorting through the best most honest way to tell it.
